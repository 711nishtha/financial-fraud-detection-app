Feature Engineering Innovations

Transforming 6 raw columns into 29 powerful fraud indicators

⚖️
balance_diff_orig

Calculates the change in origin account balance. Fraudulent transactions often show unusual balance changes compared to transaction amounts.

🎯
orig_balance_consistent

78.6% feature importance! Checks if balance change matches transaction amount. Inconsistencies are a major fraud indicator.

📊
amount_to_oldbalance_ratio

Ratio of transaction amount to origin balance. High ratios (draining accounts) correlate with fraudulent behavior.

💵
is_amount_rounded

Binary indicator for amounts rounded to $1,000. Fraudsters often use round numbers (3.69% vs 0.05% in normal transactions).

👥
c_to_c / c_to_m

Customer-to-Customer and Customer-to-Merchant transaction patterns. Different fraud rates across interaction types.

🕐
transaction_hour

Time-based feature extracted from transaction step. Fraudulent activity shows temporal patterns.
