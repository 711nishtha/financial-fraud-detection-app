Challenges Overcome

Building this system wasn't without its hurdles. Here are the key challenges I faced and how I solved them:

01

Extreme Class Imbalance

Problem: Only 0.129% of transactions were fraudulent (8,213 out of 6.3M). Traditional models would simply predict "no fraud" for everything and still achieve 99.87% accuracy.
Solution: Implemented SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Oversampling Technique) to balance the training data, used stratified cross-validation, and optimized for F1-score instead of accuracy. Applied class weights and threshold optimization for business-optimal results.
02

Feature Engineering at Scale

Problem: The raw dataset had only basic transaction data. Creating meaningful fraud indicators required domain knowledge and creative feature engineering.
Solution: Engineered 29 features including balance consistency checks, ratio-based features, rounded amount detection, and customer interaction patterns. The top feature (balance_consistent) alone contributed 78.6% to model predictions.
03

Model Selection & Tuning

Problem: Needed to benchmark multiple algorithms and find the optimal model that balanced precision, recall, and business metrics.
Solution: Trained and compared 5 algorithms (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, LightGBM). Selected Random Forest for highest F1 (99.88%) but deployed XGBoost for production due to superior threshold optimization and business savings ($2.4B vs $2.3B).
04

Business Threshold Optimization

Problem: Default 0.5 threshold wasn't optimal for business. Needed to balance fraud detection with false positive investigation costs.
Solution: Conducted comprehensive threshold analysis from 0.1 to 0.9, calculating net savings for each. Found 0.1 threshold maximized savings ($2.406B) by catching 99.8% of fraud with minimal investigation overhead.
05

Production Deployment

Problem: Moving from Jupyter notebook to production-ready web application required completely different skillset and architecture.
Solution: Built Flask backend with RESTful API, created responsive frontend with luxury fintech design, implemented proper error handling and model serving, and prepared deployment configurations for Railway/Render platforms.
